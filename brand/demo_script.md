# Zenbid — 90-Second Demo Script

**Version:** 1.0 (Foundation Sprint lock)
**Use:** Warm-network tier 1 outreach. Screen recording or live share.
**Do not ad-lib the product claims.** Every feature named below is live in production.

---

## Setup before recording

- Log in as your real account (not a test account — real project data reads better)
- Have a real PDF plan queued to upload, or use an existing project with a few assemblies
- Window: browser only, full screen, no other tabs visible
- Resolution: 1920×1080 or 2560×1440. No phone. No low-res.
- Cursor: move deliberately, pause on each UI moment

---

## The script (read/adapt — keep the structure)

### Hook (0–10s)

> "Estimators miss line items. Not because they don't know their trade — because pulling a takeoff from a PDF and turning it into a number is slow and easy to shortchange. Here's what we built."

*[Start screen recording. Land on Zenbid dashboard.]*

---

### Beat 1 — Upload and draft (10–35s)

> "I drop a plan PDF in here."

*[Click New Project. Fill in project name and location — real values, not "Test Project". Click Create. Navigate to Takeoff. Click Upload Plan.]*

> "Tally reads the plan and drafts a takeoff."

*[Upload PDF. Let Tally process. Show the draft line items appearing.]*

> "It's not perfect. It's a head start."

*[Pause on the draft. Don't rush past it.]*

---

### Beat 2 — Estimator owns the numbers (35–60s)

> "Now I go through it line by line. Anything Tally missed or got wrong — I fix it."

*[Edit 2–3 line items. Change a quantity, change a unit cost, add a line item manually.]*

> "Every correction I make stays. The system learns what my work actually costs."

*[Pause. Let that land.]*

---

### Beat 3 — Proposal out (60–80s)

> "When the estimate looks right, I generate a proposal."

*[Navigate to the project report or proposal view. Click generate/export.]*

> "Takeoff to proposal. One tool."

*[Show the output briefly — don't dwell. Clean document.]*

---

### Close (80–90s)

> "We're in early access right now — first estimators testing it, giving feedback, shaping what gets built. If you want a spot, reply to this and I'll set you up."

*[End recording on the dashboard — not a signup page.]*

---

## Voice rules for the recording

- Say "Tally" not "the AI" or "the tool"
- Say "Zenbid" not "ZenBid" or "Zen Bid"
- Do not say: AI-powered, seamless, smart, intelligent, revolutionize, disrupt, cutting-edge, next-gen, game-changer
- Do not reference roadmap features (scope gap detection, dual-costing grid, Assembly Builder polish) unless they ship before this clip records
- Do not show pricing. Do not say $29 or any dollar amount.
- Do not show fake data. If you don't have a real PDF, use a simple hand-drawn sketch — just something real.
